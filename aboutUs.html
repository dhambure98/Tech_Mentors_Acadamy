Tech Mentors Since 2018

The Tech Mentors with legacy over 05 years of service in the online higher education is focusing on resilience the professional practice gap in the fields of Management, Information Technology, Engineering, Design and Languages. Throughout this journey we have enriched thousands of students by ensuring better career opportunities in both local and foreign job markets Concurrent innovations, developments and efforts coupled with experienced professional lead Tech Menthors to differentiate itself from other online higher education service providers. Currently, Tech Mentors operates as a self-financed statuary body under the State World of Skills Development, Vocational Education, Research & Innovations. Today, we are recognized as one of the online higher educational institute to be awarded with ISO 9001-2008 which certifies that our processes and systems are according to the internationally recognized standards.

Our Mission

Every individual has the potential to create change, whether in their life, their community,
or the world. The transformative power of education is what unlocks that potential.
Yet, access to online high-quality education has been a privilege of the few. Back in 2012, we realized it
was a time for a seismic shift in online learning. From the tried and true to the leading edge.
From “for some” to “for all.” By opening the classroom through online learning, Tech Mentors empowers
millions of learners to unlock their potential and become changemakers.
